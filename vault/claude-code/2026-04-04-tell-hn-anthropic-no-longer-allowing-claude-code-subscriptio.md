Anthropic ending Claude subscription coverage for third-party harnesses like OpenClaw, requiring separate pay-as-you-go billing starting April 4

## Key Insight

Significant policy change that could increase costs for users of third-party Claude harnesses, but one-time credit and discount bundles offered to ease transition
