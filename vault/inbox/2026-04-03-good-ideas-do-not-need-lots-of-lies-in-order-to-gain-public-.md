## Summary

A principle that genuinely good ideas should gain acceptance on merit without requiring deceptive marketing or false claims

## Key Insight

Use this as a heuristic when evaluating new tools, frameworks, or technologies - be skeptical of solutions that need excessive hype rather than demonstrable value
