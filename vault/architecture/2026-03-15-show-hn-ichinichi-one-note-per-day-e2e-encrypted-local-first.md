## Summary

A minimalist daily journaling app with local-first storage, E2E encryption, and read-only past entries to prevent over-editing

## Key Insight

Making past entries read-only prevents perfectionism and improves consistency by forcing focus on today's entry rather than endlessly editing yesterday's
